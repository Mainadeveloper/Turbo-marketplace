# Turbo Marketplace

A simple storefront website for selling turbo parts to customers in Kenya — cartridges and 
complete turbo units for vehicles like the Toyota Landcruiser, 1KD engines, and Ford Ranger 2.2.

The idea behind the site is trust. Buying turbo parts online can feel risky, so instead of a 
generic product catalog, customers get real photos of the actual parts, honest explanations of 
what they're looking at (like cartridge vs. complete unit), and a direct way to reach out and 
confirm fitment and price before committing to a purchase.

## What it includes

- Real product photos, not stock images
- Simple explanations of turbo parts for customers who aren't mechanics
- Direct enquiry via phone or email — no complicated checkout
- A short "how turbos work" section for anyone curious
- SEO basics built in (meta tags, Open Graph, structured data)

## Built with

React, TypeScript, TanStack Router/Start, Vite, Tailwind CSS, and Lucide icons.
